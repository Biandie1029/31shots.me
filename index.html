About the Photographer

_31shots_ is an up and coming creative director coming out of Miami, Florida and has found his niche in advocating for underepresented communities.

How it Started

31 has always been intrigued by the idea of photography but he never took the opportunity to explore himself artistically and puruse it as a career. Throughout his college journey, 31 has been blessed with the opportunity to be part of a new student organization at the Unviversity of Miami called the Lab Miami. The Lab is a space on campus for students who are interested in photography, videography, editing and/or event coverage. Through the Lab, 31 was able to create a career shooting various student-led events on campus. After a semester of shooting, he is now expanding his interests and beginning to pursue cinematography and documentary style film.

What the Future Holds

31 is a firm beleiver in following his heart and discovering his destiny along the way. While venturing into the unknown, 31 knows that by continuing to amplify the voices of the African American community and advocating for the causes he believes in, his photos and films will make a difference all over the world.
